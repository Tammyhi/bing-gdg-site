About Us

Welcome to the Game Development Group, the coolest place on campus to dive into the world of game creation! 🎮✨ Founded in 2023, we're the first-ever game development club here, proudly part of the Video Game Association. Whether you're a newbie or a seasoned coder, our workshops and semesterly game jams are designed to spark creativity and boost your skills in a fun, supportive environment.

Join us to collaborate with fellow enthusiasts, learn the latest in game development, and create amazing games together. No experience? No problem! Our club is all about having a blast while learning and growing. Come for the games, stay for the community! 🚀🕹️

Note: We are a sub-group of the Video Game Association and not officially chartered yet. We're hoping to get charted this academic year.

Eboard

Meet our awesome Eboard! 🎉 We're the friendly faces behind the Game Development Group, here to make sure you have a fantastic time learning and creating. Each of us is passionate about games and excited to help you bring your ideas to life. From creating the graphics of our social media posts to running game jams, we're dedicated to fostering a fun and supportive community. Interested in joining the Eboard? We'd love to have you! It's a great way to get involved, share your love for game development, and make lasting friendships. 🚀🕹️
