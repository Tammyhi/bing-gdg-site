About Us

Founded in 2023 as a sub-group of the Video Game Association, we're the first-ever game development club at Binghamton University. Whether you're a newbie or a seasoned coder, our events are designed to spark creativity and boost your skills in a fun, supportive environment.

Join us to collaborate with fellow enthusiasts, learn the latest in game development, and create amazing games together. Come for the games, stay for the community!
